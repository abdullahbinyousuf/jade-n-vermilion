Our Heritage

The Artisan's
Touch

At Jade & Vermilion, we believe that jewelry is a tangible connection to history. Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by master artisans who have dedicated their lives to preserving ancient techniques.

From the selection of Grade A Jadeite to the intricate filigree work of our gold ornaments, we honor the past while designing for the future. Wearing our pieces is an embrace of luck, prosperity, and the enduring beauty of the East.
